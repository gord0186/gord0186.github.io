About

My name is Rudy Gordon; I come from a small rural area out side of Ottawa. I went to school at Opeongo High school, when I attended school there I was not to sure what I wanted to do with my life; but when one of my family members approached me and wanted me to make a crest to represent my grandpa. I did it, but when I was done and seen the final product on the jacket I new that this is what I wanted to do. To do all of that work and see the peace done and out there in the world gave me a feeling of accomplishment. From there on I have just tried making my skills better in every thing that I have done.

Now that I am a Third year graphic designer I can do far better thing then I every thought that I could do; and still get the same kind of enjoyment out of them because there is no limits to what we can do. There is a quote that I heard that I found it suit's the way that i think it is by Les Brown and he said “The only limits to the possibilities in your life tomorrow are the buts you use today.”
